What Programming Club Does

Programming club is a club at Burnside highschool. At programming club we usually learn how to program in many different programming languages. We also spend time getting ready for competitions or just program for fun! Some of the students there also spend time to continue with their tasks from digital technology classes (Finish their website, game, etc).

How to Join

How do you join Programming club? It's very simple! All you need to do is turn up to the club. The club is on each each Friday lunch time in P14. It's absolutely fine even if you are a complete begginer. Can't wait to see you there!

Competitions

If you join programming club, you will be able to gain the opportunity of joining programming competitions. The competiotions are really fun and you get to develope you programming skills.

"Competition" may sound intimidating, but it's just where you gather with people from other schools and just solve problems. Some competitions can be done at home (NZOI).
